# Flight Research: Dublin to Verona (June 2026)

This document outlines the best flight and transfer options for a family trip to Lake Garda (staying at Del Garda Village and Camping, Peschiera del Garda) for the dates **June 27, 2026 – July 7, 2026**.

**Constraints:**
*   **Passengers:** 2 Adults, 3 Children.
*   **Route:** Dublin (DUB) to Verona (VRN) preferred. Nonstop.
*   **Timing:** Depart > 7:30 AM, Arrive/Depart < 7:00 PM (to accommodate children's sleep).
*   **Dates:** Out Sat June 27 (or Fri evening June 26). Return Tue July 7 (+2 days flexibility).
*   **Goal:** Maximize time in Italy, minimize travel stress.

---

## 1. Flight Options (Direct: Dublin - Verona)

The primary carriers for this route are **Ryanair** and **Aer Lingus**.

### Schedule Status
**Note:** Ryanair has not yet released its Summer 2026 flight schedule. Based on historical trends, the schedule is expected to be released around **mid-September 2025** (approx. 9 months in advance).

### Option A: Ryanair (Best for Maximizing Time)
Ryanair typically operates frequent services during the summer schedule (often daily or near-daily), offering good flexibility for the Tuesday return.

*   **Outbound (Sat June 27):**
    *   **Flight:** FR4266
    *   **Time:** Depart DUB **~09:10 AM** – Arrive VRN **~12:45 PM**
    *   **Pros:** Perfect timing (not too early, arrive in time for lunch/afternoon swim). Maximizes the first day without an exhausting start.
    *   **Cons:** Strict baggage limits (add priority/bags for family).

*   **Inbound (Tue July 7):**
    *   **Flight:** FR4267
    *   **Time:** **Varies significantly by day of week.** Historical data from Summer 2024 shows morning departures on some days (e.g., ~11:00 AM) and afternoon on others (e.g., ~5:00 PM). Winter schedules (Jan 2026) show ~12:10 PM departures.
    *   **Note:** If Tuesday flight is unavailable or times are poor (e.g., late night), consider **Wednesday July 8** or **Thursday July 9** as flexible options.

*   **Supporting Links:**
    *   [FlightAware History - FR4266 (DUB-VRN)](https://www.flightaware.com/live/flight/RYR4266)
    *   [FlightAware History - FR4267 (VRN-DUB)](https://www.flightaware.com/live/flight/RYR4267)

### Option B: Aer Lingus (More Comfort, Fixed Schedule)
Aer Lingus operates a scheduled service, typically on Saturdays.

*   **Outbound (Sat June 27):**
    *   **Flight:** EI412
    *   **Time:** Depart DUB **~12:30 PM** – Arrive VRN **~04:05 PM**
    *   **Pros:** Very comfortable departure time. Arrive in time for dinner/evening settlement. 4-star airline service.
    *   **Cons:** Arrives later in the day than Ryanair.

*   **Inbound (Tue July 7):**
    *   **Challenge:** Aer Lingus often operates this route only on Saturdays (1x weekly).
    *   **Solution:** You may need to book a **one-way out with Aer Lingus** and return with **Ryanair** on the Tuesday/Wednesday, or extend the trip to Saturday July 11 (14 nights) to fly Aer Lingus return.

### Recommendation
**Mix & Match:** Fly **Ryanair outbound** (09:10 AM) to get the most out of the first day, and book **Ryanair return** on Tuesday/Wednesday. If you prefer Aer Lingus comfort, fly EI412 out, but be prepared to use Ryanair for the return leg on a Tuesday.

## 2. Wildcard Options

If direct Dublin-Verona flights are expensive or sold out, consider these alternatives.

### Option C: Belfast International (BFS) to Verona
**Jet2** operates a direct service on Saturdays.
*   **Time:** Depart BFS **~08:00 AM** – Arrive VRN **~11:25 AM**.
*   **Pros:** excellent arrival time; Jet2 is very family-friendly.
*   **Cons:** Travel to Belfast required; separate currency/parking logistics.

### Option D: Nearby Airports (Venice or Milan)
If dates don't align for Verona, fly into a nearby hub.
*   **Venice Marco Polo (VCE):** ~1.5 hours to Peschiera. Frequent Aer Lingus/Ryanair flights.
*   **Milan Bergamo (BGY):** ~1 hour to Peschiera. Ryanair hub.
*   **Pros:** Much higher frequency (daily flights), often cheaper.
*   **Cons:** Longer transfer to the resort.

## 3. Transfer Options (Verona Airport to Peschiera del Garda)

Peschiera del Garda is very close to Verona Airport (approx. 25km), making the transfer quick and easy.

### **Primary Recommendation: Private Transfer**
For a family of 5 with luggage, this is the most stress-free option.
*   **Provider:** Local companies like *Verona Airport Transfers* or *Welcome Pickups*.
*   **Vehicle:** Minivan (Mercedes V-Class or similar).
*   **Cost:** **€75 - €100** (approx. total one way).
*   **Time:** **~20-25 minutes**.
*   **Pros:** Door-to-door, driver waits at arrivals, car seats available on request. No hauling luggage on/off trains.

### **Secondary Option: Train Transfer**
Feasible but involves multiple legs.
1.  **Bus:** "Verona Airlink" (Bus 199) from Airport to Verona Porta Nuova Station.
    *   Freq: Every 20 mins. Time: 15 mins. Cost: €6/pp.
2.  **Train:** Verona Porta Nuova to Peschiera del Garda.
    *   Freq: Frequent (Regional/High Speed). Time: 15 mins. Cost: €4-€10/pp.
3.  **Last Mile:** Taxi/Walk from Peschiera Station to Del Garda Village (~2km).
*   **Total Cost:** ~€50 - €70 for family.
*   **Total Time:** ~1 hour+.
*   **Verdict:** Not recommended. The savings (~€30) are not worth the hassle of managing 3 kids + luggage across 3 modes of transport compared to a direct private van.
