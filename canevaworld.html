CanevaWorld Resort Map
Via Fossalta 58, 37017 Lazise (VR). Two parks side by side — Caneva Aquapark (a big tropical waterpark) and Movieland Park (movie-themed rides & stunt shows). About 4 km from Del Garda Village (~8 min by car, ~15–20 min cycling). Quieter and more relaxed than Gardaland — a great change of pace.

Plan & Hours

  • Season 2026: roughly 23 May – 13 Sep; each park has its own calendar (the Aquapark runs daily in high summer, Movieland often opens later in the day with evening shows). Confirm dates/hours on canevaworld.it.
  • Caneva Aquapark = save it for a hot, sunny day.
  • Movieland = good even on a cloudy day (shows + dry rides).

Don’t miss the stunt shows — the Hollywood Stunt Show and Police Academy show are the highlight of Movieland and are great for kids. Check show times on the map at entry and plan your day around them.

Insider Tips

  • Aquapark essentials: water shoes, towels, sun cream, a sun hat, and €1–2 coins for lockers. Sun loungers fill early — arrive at opening.
  • Swimwear rules: Italian waterparks usually require proper swimwear (no board shorts with pockets/zips on some slides) — pack briefs/trunks.
  • Cheaper than Gardaland and less crowded — a relaxed day for the family.
  • Combine smartly: a hot day = Aquapark; a cooler/cloudy day = Movieland shows.
