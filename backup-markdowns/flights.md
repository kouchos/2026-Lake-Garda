## 3. Transfer Options (Verona Airport to Peschiera del Garda)

Peschiera del Garda is very close to Verona Airport (approx. 25km), making the transfer quick and easy.

### **Primary Recommendation: Private Transfer**
For a family of 5 with luggage, this is the most stress-free option.
*   **Provider:** Local companies like *Verona Airport Transfers* or *Welcome Pickups*.
*   **Vehicle:** Minivan (Mercedes V-Class or similar).
*   **Cost:** **€75 - €100** (approx. total one way).
*   **Time:** **~20-25 minutes**.
*   **Pros:** Door-to-door, driver waits at arrivals, car seats available on request. No hauling luggage on/off trains.

### **Secondary Option: Train Transfer**
Feasible but involves multiple legs.
1.  **Bus:** "Verona Airlink" (Bus 199) from Airport to Verona Porta Nuova Station.
    *   Freq: Every 20 mins. Time: 15 mins. Cost: €6/pp.
2.  **Train:** Verona Porta Nuova to Peschiera del Garda.
    *   Freq: Frequent (Regional/High Speed). Time: 15 mins. Cost: €4-€10/pp.
3.  **Last Mile:** Taxi/Walk from Peschiera Station to Del Garda Village (~2km).
*   **Total Cost:** ~€50 - €70 for family.
*   **Total Time:** ~1 hour+.
*   **Verdict:** Not recommended. The savings (~€30) are not worth the hassle of managing 3 kids + luggage across 3 modes of transport compared to a direct private van.
