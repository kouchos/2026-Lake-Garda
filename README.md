# Family Trip to Lake Garda, Italy 🇮🇹

## Overview
This repository is dedicated to planning our family trip to Lake Garda, Italy. We will use this space to organize our itinerary, manage bookings, and keep track of expenses.

## 📅 Trip Details
- **Destination:** Lake Garda, Italy
- **Dates:** Saturday, June 27, 2026 – Sunday, July 5, 2026
- **Duration:** 9 Days, 8 Nights

## 📂 Repository Structure
We can organize the planning documents into the following folders/files:

- `/itinerary`: Daily plans and schedules.
- `/accommodation`: Hotel/Airbnb research and booking confirmations.
- `/transportation`: Flight details, car rental info, and local transport maps.
- `/activities`: Ideas for sightseeing, tours, hiking, and water sports.
- `/budget`: Spreadsheets for estimated costs and actual expenses.
- `/docs`: Important travel documents (copies of passports, insurance, etc. - *keep secure!*).
