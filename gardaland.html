Gardaland Resort Map
Via Derna 4, 37014 Castelnuovo del Garda (VR). Italy’s number-one theme park — about 7 km from Del Garda Village (~12 min by car or the seasonal Gardaland shuttle). Cycling is possible (~25–30 min) but the roads are busy, so driving or the shuttle is recommended for the family.

Plan & Hours

  • Opening (late June / early July): typically 10:00–23:00 in peak summer, with evening shows and fireworks. Confirm the daily calendar on gardaland.it before you go.
  • Best day to visit: mid-week (Tue–Thu). Weekends fill with local families. Arrive at opening and ride the big coasters first.
  • SEA LIFE Aquarium sits next to the park (separate or combo ticket; included with some season passes).

Little Ones

  • Peppa Pig Land — gentle rides for toddlers/young kids (★★).
  • DreamWorks / Kung Fu Panda area — family rides and character meets.
  • Prezzemolo Land — the park mascot’s play area, roundabouts and mini rides.
  • Shows & parades — daily; check the park map for evening fireworks in summer.

Insider Tips

  • Get measured early: kids are given a coloured wristband at the entrance height-check — saves arguing at every ride.
  • Skip the queue: Gardaland has no free single-rider line; it sells a paid “Single Ride Express” (~€3–5 per ride). A Gold pass includes 1 free Time-Saver ride per visit.
  • Beat the heat: ride the wet rides (Jungle Rapids, Colorado Boat) at midday, do indoor rides (I Corsari, Ramses, Jumanji) in the hottest hours.
  • Bring: refillable water, sun cream, a spare T-shirt for after the water rides, and cash/card for lockers (~€1–2) and snacks.
  • Money-saver: there are picnic areas — a packed lunch beats the in-park food prices for five.
