3. Getting Around Locally

  • To Peschiera Town: Walk (20 mins safe path) or use the campsite shuttle / local bus (Line 164).
  • To Gardaland: Free Shuttle Bus runs from Peschiera Station every 30 mins.
  • Supermarkets:
    • On-site: Well-stocked market for basics (bread/milk/wine).
    • Lidl / Eurospin: Located on Via Frassino (near Bella Italia), approx 10-15 min walk or quick taxi.
    • Delivery: Some larger supermarkets may offer delivery.
  • Cycling: Excellent network of flat, safe paths. The campsite has direct access to local routes.

4. Cycling Guide (Family Friendly)

  • Safety: 10/10. Peschiera is the start of the famous Mincio Cycle Path. It is flat, paved, and fully separated from cars.
  • Getting to Town: The route from Via Marzan to the centre is safe for 7-year-olds (largely pavement/path).
  • Rental Costs (Estimates):
    • Adult City Bike: ~€15/day.
    • Child Bike: ~€10/day.
    • Family Total: ~€60 - €80 for a full day.
    • E-Bikes: Available (~€30/day) if you want an easier ride.
  • Top Route: Peschiera to Borghetto (15km).
    • A stunning medieval village built on the river.
    • Completely flat, car-free path.
    • Stop for tortellini in Borghetto and cycle back.
    • Time: ~1.5 - 2 hours each way at "kid pace".

6. Taxis & Uber:

  • Uber: Exists but is "Black" (Mercedes) only. Expensive.
  • Local Taxis: Best option for short hops. Peschiera Station to Campsite is ~€15. Use the reception to call them.
