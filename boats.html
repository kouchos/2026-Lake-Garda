7. Boat Hire on Lake Garda

Getting out on the water is the highlight of a Lake Garda holiday. From Peschiera del Garda Map (and nearby Lazise, Bardolino and Garda) you can hire anything from a pedalo for an hour to a self-drive motorboat for a full day — no licence needed for the smaller engines.

Where to Hire

  • Peschiera del Garda Map — rental kiosks line the canal/harbour and the lakefront; closest to the resort.
  • Lazise Map — pretty walled town ~10 min north; several motorboat rentals on the harbour.
  • Bardolino & Garda Map — more choice a little further up the eastern shore.
  • Beach kiosks at Pacengo/Lido di Lazise for pedalos, SUP and kayaks.

Operator names change season to season — search “noleggio barche senza patente Peschiera” (boat hire without licence) and check recent reviews and the fuel/deposit policy before booking.

Half-Day vs Full-Day vs an Hour

  • An hour — perfect for a pedalo or SUP near the beach, or a quick electric-boat pootle to let the kids “drive.”
  • Half-day (morning) — the sweet spot for a family motorboat: head out before the wind picks up, find a quiet bay to swim, back for lunch.
  • Full day — only if you want to cruise far (e.g. towards Sirmione, Punta San Vigilio). Factor in fuel, heat and tired children.

Great Spots to Aim For

  • Sirmione Map — anchor off the shallow “Jamaica Beach” below the Roman ruins for a swim.
  • Punta San Vigilio Map — a postcard headland with a sheltered bay.
  • Isola del Garda — the lake’s only island; admire it from the water.

Safety & Practical Tips

  • Life jackets for the children are mandatory — the operator provides them; insist on a good fit.
  • Watch the wind: Garda’s southern basin can get choppy in the afternoon (the Ora wind). Go in the morning for calm water.
  • Storms: afternoon thunderstorms blow up fast — check the forecast and head in early if the sky darkens.
  • Bring: sun cream, hats, water, a dry bag for phones, and cash/card for the deposit.
  • Fuel is rarely included on motorboats — ask how it’s charged before you set off.
  • No-licence limit: engines up to ~40 hp can be driven without a boat licence; the operator briefs you on the basics before departure.

Prefer to Be a Passenger?

The public Navigazione Laghi ferries Map run from Peschiera to Sirmione, Lazise, Bardolino, Garda and beyond — a cheap, relaxed way to reach the pretty towns without driving a boat yourself.
