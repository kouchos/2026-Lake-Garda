A change of pace from theme parks and pools: a safari park, one of Italy’s most beautiful gardens, and a storybook medieval village on the river — all within a short drive (or, for the village, a bike ride) of the resort. Great for a calmer family day.

Parco Natura Viva

Parco Natura Viva Map (Bussolengo) is a wildlife park in two halves: a drive-through safari (lions, giraffes, rhinos — seen from your car) and a walk-through zoo with a dinosaur trail the kids love.

  • Plan: half to a full day. Do the safari early when animals are active.
  • Without a car: the drive-through needs a vehicle — ask the park about its safari shuttle/bus option if you’re car-free. (Confirm before going.)
  • Bring: sun protection, water and a picnic (there are cafe/picnic areas).
  • Tickets/hours: book online; prices and opening days to be verified on the park site.

Parco Giardino Sigurtà

Sigurtà Garden Park Map (Valeggio sul Mincio) is a huge, beautifully landscaped garden — lawns, lakes, a maze and seasonal flower displays. Summer brings the water-lily ponds and roses.

  • Get around in style: rent a golf cart or hop on the little road-train — a winner with children across the big site.
  • Kids’ bits: a hedge maze, a tortoise/petting area and lots of room to run.
  • Plan: a relaxed half-day; go in the morning before the heat peaks.
  • Tickets/hours: verify on the park site — it’s open daily in summer.

Borghetto sul Mincio & Valeggio

Borghetto sul Mincio Map is a tiny medieval village of watermills strung along the river — one of the prettiest corners near the lake, and the home of tortellini di Valeggio (“love knots”, see Food & Drink).

  • By bike: it’s the classic destination on the flat, traffic-free Mincio cycle path from Peschiera — the route and timings are on the Bike Hire page. Stop for lunch, then roll back.
  • By car: ~25 min; combine with Sigurtà (they’re side by side).
  • Don’t miss: the views from the Visconti bridge and a riverside lunch.

Closer to Home

  • SEA LIFE Aquarium sits next to Gardaland (separate or combo ticket) — a good indoor option on a hot or stormy afternoon.
  • The Mincio river path & lake promenade are nature on the doorstep — great for an easy family cycle or a morning run.

Opening days, transport options and prices flagged for verification — check each park’s website close to your visit.
